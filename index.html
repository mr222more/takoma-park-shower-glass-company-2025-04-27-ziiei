About Us

Founded in 2005, Takoma Park Shower Glass Company began with a vision to provide premium custom glass solutions to homeowners in the DC metro area. What started as a small family business has grown into the area's most trusted name in shower glass installation and design.

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge glass technology with exceptional craftsmanship to create stunning, durable shower enclosures that enhance the beauty and value of your home. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.
